**LED Blink Project (KiCad)**

I designed a simple LED blinking circuit using KiCad to understand the basics of electronic circuit design and PCB development. The project involved creating the schematic, selecting components, assigning footprints, and designing the PCB layout. I also routed the connections and performed design checks to ensure the circuit was ready for fabrication. Through this project, I gained practical experience with KiCad, PCB design principles, and electronic hardware development.
